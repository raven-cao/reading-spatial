Immune System

T Cell States

Effector vs. exhausted — the difference that determines tumor fate

The CD8+ T cell's job

Cytotoxic T lymphocytes (CD8+) are the immune system's killers. Origin: bone marrow → thymus maturation (hence "T"). Function: recognize abnormal cells via TCR, approach, release perforin (punches holes in target membrane) and granzyme B (enters through holes, triggers apoptosis). Also secrete IFN-γ to recruit other immune cells.

Effector state — armed and fighting

  • GZMB (granzyme B) — high: loaded weapon
  • PRF1 (perforin) — high: delivery mechanism for granzyme
  • IFNG (interferon-γ) — high: calling for backup
  • TNF — high: direct pro-inflammatory signal
  • PD-1, TIM-3 — low: brakes off

Exhausted state — alive but disarmed

  • PDCD1 (encodes PD-1) — high: brake pedal oversized and sensitive
  • HAVCR2 (encodes TIM-3) — high: second brake system
  • CTLA4, TIGIT — high: third and fourth brakes
  • TOX — high: the master transcription factor that locks in exhaustion
  • GZMB, IFNG — low: weapons holstered
Exhaustion is active, not passive
Exhaustion isn't cellular damage or death. It's an adaptive program — cells chronically stimulated without resolution activate TOX, which epigenetically reprograms gene expression, locking the cell into a low-reactivity state. This prevents autoimmune damage from runaway T cell activity. From the tumor's perspective, it's a feature, not a bug.

Why exhaustion is partially irreversible

TOX-driven epigenetic changes (chromatin remodeling, DNA methylation patterns) are stable and self-reinforcing, like cell differentiation states. PD-1 inhibitors remove one brake but can't reverse the underlying epigenetic reprogramming. Deeply exhausted T cells may not recover full effector function even after checkpoint blockade — relevant to why late-stage MSS tumors often don't respond to immunotherapy.

The key question for the CRC project

In MSI tumors, what signals in the microenvironment are slowing or preventing this exhaustion program? That's what AMICI's attention weights are designed to reveal.
